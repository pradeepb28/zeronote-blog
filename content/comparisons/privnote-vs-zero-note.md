## What Privnote does well

Privnote has been around for years and is widely used for one-time secret sharing.

Here’s what it does well:

- Extremely simple to use — no setup required  
- Works entirely in the browser  
- One-time read notes with automatic destruction  
- Familiar and trusted by many users  

👉 **Best for:** Quick, no-friction sharing when simplicity is enough  

---

## Where Privnote starts to feel limited

As your needs grow, some limitations become noticeable:

- **No configurable view limits** — notes are strictly read-once with no multi-view options  
- **Basic email-only notifications** — lacks instant push alerts or integrated in-app tracking  
- **No location or access restrictions** — links can be opened from any region or IP once shared  
- **No history or audit trail** — cannot manage or revoke active links after creation  
- **No mobile-native experience** — operates entirely inside a mobile web browser  
- **Ad-supported interface** — relies on 3rd-party ads to remain free  

These aren’t issues for basic use — but they matter when you need more control.
